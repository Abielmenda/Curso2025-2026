2) Licensing of the data source and potential license

The datasets provided by Transports Metropolitans de Barcelona (TMB) are published under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license.

This license allows copying, redistribution, and adaptation of the data, including for commercial use, as long as proper attribution is given to the original source.

Derived works can also be released under CC BY 4.0 to maintain legal compatibility and encourage data reuse.

3) Resource naming strategy

The URI design follows this pattern:

  • Domain: https://data.example.org/transport/bcn/
  • Path: tmb/ (project or dataset identifier)
  • Ontology terms path: https://data.example.org/transport/bcn/tmb/ontology/
  • Individuals path: https://data.example.org/transport/bcn/tmb/resource/

Rules:

  • Use # (hash) URIs for ontological terms (classes and properties).
  • Use / (slash) URIs for individuals (instances).

Examples:

  • Ontology term: https://data.example.org/transport/bcn/tmb/ontology/Station#hasLine
  • Individual: https://data.example.org/transport/bcn/tmb/resource/Station/SagradaFamilia

This approach ensures clear separation between ontology definitions and data instances, promotes reusability, and maintains semantic consistency across datasets.
