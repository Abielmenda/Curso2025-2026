Dataset Requirements — Group 08

Selected Datasets

  1. Air Quality in Barcelona — measurements of air pollutants across monitoring stations in the city.
  2. Traffic Street Sections with Weather Forecast — traffic intensity and weather conditions in real time with 15-minute predictions.
  3. Parking Service for Bikes in Barcelona — geolocated public bike parking facilities and their capacities.

Conclusions

The selected datasets fulfill all mandatory requirements (R1–R4) and partially satisfy optional ones (R5–R6). Together they cover complementary aspects of urban life — environmental quality, mobility, and infrastructure — forming an ideal foundation for building a smart city application that explores the relationship between traffic, pollution, and sustainable transport.
