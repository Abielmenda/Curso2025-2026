Since license texts are authoritative only on the source portal, please confirm on the dataset landing pages. Appendix — Notes for follow-up (URIs & Ontology)

The group will use the base domain http://group24.linkeddata.es/ for future steps. A typical pattern is:

  • Ontology terms: http://group24.linkeddata.es/ontology/ClassOrProperty
  • Instances (resources): http://group24.linkeddata.es/resource/<dataset>/<local-id>

Ontology design and a sample RDF instantiation will be provided in ontology/ontology.ttl and ontology/ontology-example.ttl as part of the Hands-On 2 deliverables.
